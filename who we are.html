Yasmin Belo-Osagie

Yasmin Belo-Osagie aka Dj Yasbo is a cofounder of She Leads
Africa. Before She Leads Africa, she worked as a management
consultant with McKinsey and Co. She has a BA from Princeton
University, a JD from Harvard Law School and an MBA from
Stanford Business School.

Afua Osei

Afua Osei aka DJ Fufu is a cofounder of She Leads Africa. Before
She Leads Africa, she worked as a management consultant with
McKinsey and Co. Afua completed an MBA and an MPA at
University of Chicago studied international management at the
London School of Economics, and graduated cum laude from
Allegheny College.
