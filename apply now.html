Are you a young Nigerian woman looking to launch a business, advance your career, or gain valuable skills? The SLA BoostHer Program is here to help you take the next big step toward success!

The SLA BoostHer Program, in collaboration with Jobberman is a transformative initiative designed to empower young Nigerian women entrepreneurs. This program addresses the unique challenges faced by women in business and job-seeking through a blended learning experience that includes advanced self-paced courses. Through a blended learning approach, participants will gain access to high-quality training, expert-led masterclasses, and hands-on guidance tailored to their needs, fostering sustainable business growth and economic independence.

Young Nigerian Women between the ages of 18 and 35 years.
Young women with innovative business concepts and others with the potential to learn skills that would point them in the right direction of turning inherent skills to money making businesses
Young women at different stages of their career journey, including:


  • Students at the point of admission who want to gain career readiness skills.
  • Current undergraduates looking to build their employability and professional experience.
  • Recent graduates navigating the transition into the job market.

Monthly Enrollment – Join at a time that works best for you!
Self-Paced Digital Learning – Access expert-led courses anytime, anywhere.
Live Weekly Masterclasses – Learn from top industry leaders and professionals.
Exclusive Networking & Mentorship – Connect with like-minded women and mentors.
On-Campus & Community Engagements – Meet us in person at universities and remote locations!:


How You’ll Learn:

  • Interactive digital courses on SLA’s learning platform
  • Engaging live classes via Zoom & Google Meet
  • Community networking through WhatsApp & Facebook
  • Special in-person sessions for hands-on learning

By joining the SLA BoostHer Program, you gain access to life-changing opportunities designed to help you succeed in business and your career. Here’s what you’ll get:

  • Expert-Led Training – Learn directly from top industry professionals through self-paced courses and live masterclasses.
  • Mentorship & Networking – Connect with successful entrepreneurs, business leaders, and fellow participants for guidance and support.
  • Business & Career Growth – Gain practical business ideation, branding, financial management, leadership, and career development skills
  • Live Masterclasses – Participate in interactive training sessions with experts sharing real-world strategies for success.
  • Exclusive Business & Job Opportunities – Access funding opportunities, internships, job placements, and business support.
  • Flexible & Accessible Learning – Learn at your own pace through SLA’s digital platform, with additional in-person sessions at universities and community
    centers.
  • Grants & Funding Support – Get a chance to secure funding for your business through program grants and business pitch opportunities.
  • A Community of Changemakers – Join a vibrant network of ambitious, like-minded women working towards success and economic independence.
This is your chance to take control of your future! Apply now and start your journey toward personal and professional success.
